| mable eligibility | 77 | 7.4 | Related |
| aged care package eligibility | 99 | 33.8 | Related |

Note: "quiz" keyword has ZERO competition in GSC - untapped opportunity

### Tool Functionality:
- 5-7 question interactive quiz
- Questions: Age, residency, disability permanence, functional impact, support needs
- Progress indicator
- Result: Likely eligible / May be eligible / Unlikely eligible
- Next steps guidance
- Link to apply or get help

### Quiz Questions:
1. Are you under 65 years old?
2. Are you an Australian citizen or permanent resident?
3. Do you have a permanent disability (physical, intellectual, sensory, cognitive, or psychosocial)?
4. Is your disability likely to be lifelong or last more than 2 years?
5. Does your disability substantially impact your daily activities?
6. Do you need support from others or equipment to do everyday tasks?
