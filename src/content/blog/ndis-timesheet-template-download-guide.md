## NDIS Timesheet Template: Key Points
- NDIS timesheets must accurately record all support hours with correct support item numbers for proper invoicing and participant budget tracking.
- Both support workers and participants (or their representatives) must sign timesheets to verify services delivered.
- Timesheets should be completed daily or immediately after each shift while details are fresh and accurate.
- Accurate timesheet records are essential for NDIS Commission audits and demonstrate accountability in service delivery.
