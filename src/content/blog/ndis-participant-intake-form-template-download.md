## NDIS Participant Intake Form: Key Points
- Intake forms gather comprehensive information about participants including personal details, NDIS plan information, support needs, and preferences.
- Thorough intake assessments ensure services are tailored to individual needs, communication styles, and cultural requirements.
- Intake forms must include consent for information sharing, privacy acknowledgments, and emergency contact details.
- Well-completed intake forms provide the foundation for service agreements, risk assessments, and personalized support delivery.
