2.  **Step 2: Shop for Fresh Ingredients**

3.  **Step 3: Cook in Your Kitchen**
    Your personal cook prepares your meals right in your own kitchen. They can prepare a single meal or cook in batches for the days ahead. The choice is yours.

4.  **Step 4: Clean Everything Up**
