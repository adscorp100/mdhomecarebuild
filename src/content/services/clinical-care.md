**How much does 24/7 nursing care at home cost?**

Providers deliver transparent quotes after assessing your specific needs, and help you maximise your available funding.

**Can providers care for someone with dementia or Alzheimer's?**
Yes. Nurses and care teams have specialised training in dementia care, including managing behaviours of concern, maintaining safety, and providing compassionate support. They create individualised care plans that respect the person's dignity and established routines.

**What qualifications do the nurses have?**
All nursing staff are Registered Nurses (RNs) or Enrolled Nurses (ENs) with current AHPRA registration. They have completed relevant clinical training and undergo ongoing professional development. All staff also have NDIS Worker Screening clearances and police checks.

**How does in-home nursing coordinate with my GP and specialists?**
Providers maintain open communication with your entire healthcare team. Nurses can attend GP appointments virtually or provide detailed reports. They implement care plans prescribed by specialists and flag any concerns for early intervention. This coordinated approach helps prevent hospital readmissions.

**Can providers help with palliative care at home?**
Yes. Experienced nursing teams provide compassionate end-of-life care focused on comfort, dignity, and quality of life. They work closely with palliative care specialists and can coordinate with hospice services to ensure comprehensive support for both the individual and their family.
