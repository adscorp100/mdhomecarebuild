## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does plan management cost me?**
Nothing. Plan management is fully funded by the NDIS. The funding comes from a separate budget line (Improved Life Choices) and doesn't reduce your other supports.

**Can I change plan managers?**
Yes, you can switch plan managers at any time. We make the transition easy if you're coming from another provider.

**What's the difference between plan management and support coordination?**
Plan management handles your **financial administration**—invoices, payments, and budget tracking. Support coordination helps you **find and connect with services**. They're different supports that work well together.

**Do I lose control of my plan?**
No. You have full control over which providers you use and what services you receive. We just handle the financial side.

**Can I use unregistered providers?**
Yes! This is one of the biggest benefits. With plan management, you can use both NDIS-registered and unregistered providers, giving you much more choice.
