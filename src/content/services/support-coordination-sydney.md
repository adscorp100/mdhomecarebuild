## What is NDIS Support Coordination?

NDIS Support Coordination helps you implement your NDIS plan by:
- Connecting you with service providers in your area
- Building your support network and capacity
- Strengthening your ability to coordinate supports
- Managing crises and complex situations
- Monitoring your plan and budget

**Support Coordination is funded under Capacity Building (CB) Supports** in your NDIS plan (Line item 07_001_0106_1_1).

### Support Coordination Levels Explained

**Level 1: Support Connection** ($52.40/hour - 2026 rate)
- Basic help connecting with providers
- Suitable for straightforward plans
- Short-term assistance (typically 3-6 months)
- Helps you build confidence managing your own supports

**Level 2: Support Coordination** ($129.59/hour - 2026 rate)
- Ongoing coordination of multiple providers
- Help resolving service delivery issues
- Budget monitoring and plan reviews
- Most common level (60% of participants)
- Builds your capacity to manage supports long-term

**Level 3: Specialist Support Coordination** ($194.39/hour - 2026 rate)
- Complex needs or challenging circumstances
- Multi-agency coordination required
- Crisis intervention and risk management
- Requires specialist qualifications (Social Work, Psychology, OT)
- High-intensity support for complex situations

**MD Home Care provides all three levels** across Sydney and Melbourne.
